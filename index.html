About Me

I am passionate about content strategy and learner-centered design. I believe design is a powerful tool to promote social justice and enact change.

 

As a student of Human-Computer Interaction at DePaul, I am particularly interested in promoting accessibility through universal design. Currently, I'm learning how to design educational experiences for surgeons! I'm invested in designing content that is digestible to a wide audience, keeping this in mind above all else: I am not my learner, and I must work to understand them.
